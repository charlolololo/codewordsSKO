Week 1

Coding has always been an area that I did my best to avoid, as I have always been weak in areas
related to technology. However, this year I made sure to choose studios heavily surrounding new
technology to challenge myself. Thus, I knew I had to choose this in Semester 2 when I saw the
introduction and website from Codewords 2020.

What is creative coding?

Initially, I had no idea what to expect as my only impression of creative coding came from the
Codewords 2020 website. I tried searching for examples on my own but did not even know what
kind of key words to use. The examples of creative coding given by Andy really helped to set a better
perspective of what exactly we could create with coding. While the whole concept still seems rather
abstract to me, I now have more ideas on what one can create through coding.

One of the creatives introduced that left an impression on me was Winnie Soon from Hong Kong.
As a Singaporean-Chinese, designs with Chinese characters often evoke a sense of nostalgia in me
as it reminds me of home. Upon a closer look, I realised that I had chanced upon her work last year in ACCA!
